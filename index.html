About Me

I hold an M.A. in Sociology from Freie Universität Berlin. My research explores how media narratives frame artificial intelligence, focusing on power asymmetries, risk framing, and collective sentiment. I have peer-reviewed publications on hacker activism and the control society. My interests lie at the intersection of technology studies, the sociology of emotions, and the politics and societal implications of AI.
