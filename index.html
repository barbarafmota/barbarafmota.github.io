About Me

I hold a master's degree in Sociology from Freie Universität Berlin and my research explores media narratives on artificial intelligence, focusing on power asymmetries, risks and sentiments. I have published articles on hacker activism and the surveillance society, and my research interests include digital sociology, digital democracy, the politics of AI and AI and society.
